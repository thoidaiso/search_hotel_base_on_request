  Hotel Description

Renovated in 2013, the Signature Saigon Hotel guarantees guests a pleasant stay whether in Ho Chi Minh City for business or pleasure. Set 0.1 Km from the excitement of the city, this 3-star hotel commands an excellent location and provides access to the city's biggest attractions. A well-kempt environment and its proximity to Fine Arts Museum, Antique Street – Le Cong Kieu, Hoi Truong Dien Hong give to this hotel a special charm.

Offering hotel guests superior services and a broad range of amenities, Signature Saigon Hotel is committed to ensuring that your stay is as comfortable as possible. While lodging at this wonderful property, guests can enjoy currency exchange, bicycle rental, 24-hour front desk, car park, business center.

All guest accommodations feature thoughtful amenities to ensure an unparalleled sense of comfort. Besides, the hotel's host of recreational offerings ensures you have plenty to do during your stay. Signature Saigon Hotel is your one-stop destination for quality hotel accommodations in Ho Chi Minh City.
