  Hotel Description

Ideal for fun and relaxation, Royal Lotus Hotel Saigon – Managed by H&K Hospital is located in the District 1 - Japanese Area area of Ho Chi Minh City. The excitement of the city center is only 0.Km away. For those of you who want to venture out, Consulate of Slovakia, Infant Hospital 2, Nha Hat Thanh Pho (Opera House) are just some of the attractions available to visitors.

Royal Lotus Hotel Saigon – Managed by H&K Hospital also offers many facilities to enrich your stay in Ho Chi Minh City. To name a few of the hotel's facilities, there are car park, 24-hour room service, restaurant, tours, meeting facilities.

Hotel accommodations have been carefully appointed to the highest degree of comfort and convenience, with in room safe, satellite/cable TV, non smoking rooms, bathtub, television in each room. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or are just looking for a way to unwind after a hard day, you will be entertained by top-class recreational facilities such as fitness center, spa, massage, sauna. Enjoy unparalleled services and a truly prestigious address at the Royal Lotus Hotel Saigon – Managed by H&K Hospital.

  Hotel Policies

A marriage certificate is required, according to regulation from the Ministry of Public Security, if a foreigner wishes to share a room with a Vietnamese national. Otherwise, the hotel may reject the booking or request that a second room be booked.
