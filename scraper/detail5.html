  Hotel Description

Ideally situated in the heart of Ho Chi Minh city, it's within easy reach to shopping venues, business centers, airline offices and banks. The Saigon riverside is just a few minutes walk and the Tan Son Nhat International Airport is a short 15 minutes taxi ride. Each room provided comes tastefully decorated and fitted with modern facilities such as satellite TV and individual air-conditioning. Dining options onsite serves authentic Vietnamese, Chinese, European dishes. As for large events and parties, the spacious banquet room can seat up to 450 people indoors and 200 people outdoors. Further conveniences include laundry service, car rental and sauna & massage.
