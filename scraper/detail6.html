About Saigon Domaine Luxury Residences

Established in 2001, Saigon Domaine Luxury Residences is the ideal choice for those searching for peace and quiet in a natural environment with all the comfort and luxury of a private home.

The Domaine's private residences were designed to achieve seamless balance between classic simplicity and contemporary luxury, whilst making the most of the complex's natural surroundings.

Saigon Domaine Luxury Residences offers spacious serviced apartments and elegant hotel suites. Its location on Thanh Da green island in Ho Chi Minh city provides a unique environment with daily free shuttles by bus and speed boat within 15 minutes to & from city center ( destination at Bach Dang jetty, Me Linh square, on Ton Duc Thang street, district 1, Ho Chi Minh city ) and Wifi in apartments and public areas. All hotel suites & apartments are designed with the highest quality and elegance.

45 units from 72 sqm for 1-bedroom to 377 sqm for 4-bedroom, offering a variety of layouts, each of them having large sliding windows that open onto spacious terraces or loggias with breathtaking view over Saigon River and countryside.
Saigon Domaine has recently become a member of The Prestige Hotels of the World, a selection of the world's finest and most prestigious hotels.

Surrounded by a natural beauty and combining peace, elegance and tradition, this first-class city resort guarantee an unforgettable stay.
