  Hotel Description

Ideal for fun and relaxation, Villa Song Saigon (Saigon River Villa) is located in the District 2 - Thao Dien area of Ho Chi Minh City. The hotel lies 8 km from the city center and provides accessibility to important town facilities. A haven of rest and relaxation, the hotel will offer total renewal just steps away from the city's numerous attractions such as Thao Dien, Binh Quoi Tourist Area, Consulate of Switzerland.

Offering hotel guests superior services and a broad range of amenities, Villa Song Saigon (Saigon River Villa) is committed to ensuring that your stay is as comfortable as possible. This hotel offers numerous on-site facilities to satisfy even the most discerning guest.

Step into one of 23 inviting rooms and escape the stresses of the day with a wide range of amenities such as internet access – wireless, air conditioning, DVD/CD player, shared bathroom, internet access – LAN (complimentary). Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or are just looking for a way to unwind after a hard day, you will be entertained by top-class recreational facilities such as outdoor pool, massage, garden, spa, fitness center. Discover an engaging blend of professional service and a wide array of features at Villa Song Saigon (Saigon River Villa).

Great stay

The rooms and beds were superb, very comfortable and the hotel being in a residential part of the city along the Saigon river we had a wonderful restful time.
9
Everything was very well thought of, the room was very comfortable and the swimming pool nested in the gardens of the hotel was large enough to allow swimming but also having a very relaxing time. The river side setting with the colonial building makes for a spectacular setting whether one is enjoying the cool morning breeze sipping the traditional Vietnamese coffee or at night when the lighting and atmosphere turns romantic. We could tell the hotel was still in its pre-opening period as the service was not 100% perfect yet but what the staff lacked in perfection was made up by their great and warm hospitality and genuine attention to service. This is like a mini resort vacation spot while being close to the bustling city center of Ho Chi Minh City. We will surely go back soon.

Phyllis D. Couples United States of America, July 8, 2012
Stay away from room 207

Nice river view. Close to stores in District 2.
7.3
We were taken to room 207 - it's rather small with a large balcony which was fine. It was odor that turned us off. There is mold in the room and big water stains on 2 walls. This room needs to be overhauled before it is used by a guest. We wanted another room and they showed us 2 with a river view but no balcony. We asked for Room 206 which had the balcony, river view and bay windows. They would only give us a reduced rate for one night and we had to pay full price for the second night. Granted - it was a 2 room suite. Very nice room with comfortable bed. There are no blackout curtains so be prepared for a sun wake-up. The breakfast was really nice and we had a good sandwich for lunch. Did not eat dinner there. We tried the Vietnamese place in the village and I felt it was overpriced for what we received. The hotel is charming, the front desk ladies were great and spoke English. I don't have any other complaints. It rained most of the time so we did take their ferry to District 1.
