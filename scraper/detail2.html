  Hotel Description

As it is freshly built in 2013, The Luxe Hotel is a lifestyle hotel ideal whether for business or leisure travelers who seek for a modern, stylish living. Centrally located in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, The Luxe Hotel is in a nearby reach from shopping malls, department stores and many attractions like Ben Thanh Market, City Hall, Opera House, Reunification Palace, Notre-Dam Cathedral and many more. Gastronomic dining options as well as vibrant nightlife of Ho Chi Minh are easy to access. After a long day, guests can recharge themselves with newly launched guestrooms furnished with large screen LED TV, High speed internet, mini bar, 100% cotton bed linen, duet, down pillows, which all offer a new level of experience of comfort. Whatever the reason the stay is, let The Luxe Hotel make this trip the most remarkable and convenient one.
