  Hotel Description

The historic hotel in the heart of Ho Chi Minh is a classic blend of the old world and eastern charm. The Rex Hotel Saigon has long been a favorite hotel among visitors to Ho Chi Minh not only because of its central location close to the city’s many attractions and restaurant scene, but also because of its stunning architecture and quirky decor. The Rex Hotel has recently undergone a complete refurbishment but still manages to maintain its unique charm. The hotel is located right in the city center and offers guests easy access to some of the city’s best restaurants and shops, including the famous Ben Thanh Market. The rooftop restaurant offers stunning views of the entire city and is a favorite hangout spot for most guests here. If you’re looking for the most convenient choice in town offering great value for money, look no further than Rex Hotel Saigon.

  Food, drink and entertainment

The Paradise Coffee Lounge
With daily live background piano music, the Paradise Coffee Lounge – located on the ground floor – is the ideal meeting place for a relaxed coffee, afternoon tea or business meetings.
The Hoa Mai restaurant
The Hoa Mai restaurant is the perfect setting for an unforgettable dining experience. Here you’ll savor the finest Vietnamese cuisine and other Asian delectable while experiencing traditional folk music and dance shows.
Rose Garden
The Rose Garden features a tropical garden and waterfall. Relax with a coffee or indulge in a cocktail or sample from our international menu.
Rooftop Garden
Overlooking the very heart of the city, the Rooftop Garden is the place that the memories still remain, of which it recalls the world to its past famous calling “Five O’clock Follies”, where international correspondents covered the Vietnam war, U.S officers and GIs had their drinking niche during the 60s.
