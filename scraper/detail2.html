  Hotel Description

Ideally located right in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, it's within a close range to popular attractions such as Ben Thanh Handicraft Market, Mariamman Temple, Saigon Notre-Dame Basilica and Saigon Opera House. Taking merely a minute to the downtown area, discerning business travelers can find many commercial buildings and international consulates. Each room comes tastefully decorated with a modern design, offering all of the basic as well as the latest in facilities. To facilitate any meetings and seminars, the property provides fully-equipped rooms of varying sizes depending on the occasion. As for dining, the restaurant on the top floor offers a perfect blend of Vietnamese and Western specialties, complemented with a panoramic view of the city.
