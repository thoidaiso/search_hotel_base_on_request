  Hotel Description

Sunflower Central Hotel, located in District 1 - Ben Thanh Market, Ho Chi Minh City, is a popular choice for travelers. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. No less exceptional is the hotel's easy access to the city's myriad attractions and landmarks, such as Consulate of Korea, Mariamman Hindu temple, Ben Thanh Market.

The facilities and services provided by Sunflower Central Hotel ensure a pleasant stay for guests. A selection of top-class facilities such as tours, family room, coffee shop, concierge, elevator can be enjoyed at the hotel.

Sunflower Central Hotel is home to 70 bedrooms, all tastefully furnished to provide comforts such as internet access – wireless (complimentary), coffee/tea maker, shower, satellite/cable TV, whirlpool bathtub. The hotel's hot tub are ideal places to relax and unwind after a busy day. Whatever your purpose of visit, Sunflower Central Hotel is an excellent choice for your stay in Ho Chi Minh City.
