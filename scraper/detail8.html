  Hotel Description

For travelers who want to take in the sights and sounds of Ho Chi Minh City, Saigon Pink 2 Hotel is the perfect choice. Only 0.1 km from the city center, the hotel's strategic location ensures that guests can quickly and easily reach many local points of interest. Also within easy reach are Ben Thanh Market, Mariamman Hindu temple, Saigon General Hospital.

Offering hotel guests superior services and a broad range of amenities, Saigon Pink 2 Hotel is committed to ensuring that your stay is as comfortable as possible. Business center, Wi-Fi in public areas, concierge, laundry service, tours are just a few of the facilities that set Saigon Pink 2 Hotel apart from other hotels in the city.

Guests can choose from 23 rooms, all of which exude an atmosphere of total peace and harmony. Besides, the hotel's host of recreational offerings ensures you have plenty to do during your stay. Superb facilities and an excellent location make the Saigon Pink 2 Hotel the perfect base from which to enjoy your stay in Ho Chi Minh City.
