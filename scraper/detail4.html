  Hotel Description

This elegant property is ideal for those in search of a private serviced apartment that is designed to accommodate executives as well as travelers. A refreshing alternative to a hotel, The Somerset Ho Chi Minh City offers a resort-style retreat right in the heart of the city with 165 tasteful rooms. Boasting a comfortable ambiance and modern amenities, guests can enjoy all that HCMC has to offer and come back to a place they could call home. Enviably situated in the business and shopping district (District 1), the residence is close to business destinations, consulates, shopping centers, the Botanic Gardens, and a wide variety of dining and entertainment. Whether in the city for business or leisure, The Somerset Ho Chi Minh City Serviced Residence is an ideal place to stay in the spacious elegance of private residential apartments.

Relaxing holiday spoiled by errant taxi-drivers

Very spacious room, well-equipped kitchen and close to good restaurants
6.7
The stay was relaxing and the living room was spacious. However, the house-keeping staff were not up to standard, spending less than 15 minutes to clean everyday, and the towels were soggy. The bars and children playground nearby were very noisy, and we had to close the balcony all the time until 10.30pm. There were a few good restaurants nearby, and the Ocean Palace offered excellent Cantonese cuisine. Over 7 days, we met two taxi-drivers who took us for a ride, charging 600,000D from airport instead of 120,000D, with meters on. Another drove us in the opposite direction after dinner, until we had to stop him and get out of the car. We afraid he might want to rob us. Our hotel receptionist was reliable, but not the airport staff. Even the so-called reputable taxis can be dangerous.
