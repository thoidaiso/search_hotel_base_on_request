  Hotel Description

Whether you're a tourist or traveling on business, Mifuki Boutique Hotel is a great choice for accommodation when visiting Ho Chi Minh City. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

Offering hotel guests superior services and a broad range of amenities, Mifuki Boutique Hotel is committed to ensuring that your stay is as comfortable as possible. To name a few of the hotel's facilities, there are laundry service/dry cleaning, restaurant, safety deposit boxes, coffee shop, car park.

33 rooms spread over 8 floors provide a warm and pleasant home away from home and modern comforts such as internet access – wireless (complimentary), internet access – wireless, bathrobes, satellite/cable TV, shower. The hotel's recreational facilities, which include spa, massage are designed for escape and relaxation. Discover an engaging blend of professional service and a wide array of features at Mifuki Boutique Hotel.

Good value hotel: Price, quiet, location.

Price, quiet, location
9.3
I booked this hotel for one night before departure. The price was very interesting for 30$. This hotel has standard facilities for 3 stars hotel in mid range price. We had a good night there. The hotel should improve the quality of breakfast and staff performance. There are little choice for breakfast and I rarely see the employers' smiles with customers. Only one man who speaks English smiled when he asking for passport. However, I got what I paid for and still recommend for people who want a quite night, safe and good location hotel in HCM city.

10
We were blown away by the beauty of this hotel on arrival, so majestic yet very elegant. The staffs were very professional despite lack of good english, they did every best to understand and cater to our needs which was highly satisfying. Our room was just superb, comfort at every corner from cosy bed to clean bathroom , towels and all. The most basic needs for travellers like us, who just want a roof under our head , a clean linen to sleep on and a hot shower . We do not ask for more.
