  Hotel Description

An Lam Saigon River Private Residence, located in District 12 - Riverside, Ho Chi Minh City, is a popular choice for travelers. The hotel lies 18. Km from the city center and provides accessibility to important town facilities. With the city's main attractions such as Vu Anh Hospital, 175 Military Hospital, Gia Dinh Park within close reach, visitors to the hotel will just love its location.

The facilities and services provided by An Lam Saigon River Private Residence ensure a pleasant stay for guests. Guests of the hotel can enjoy on-site features like airport transfer, concierge, car park, family room, bar/pub.

The ambiance of An Lam Saigon River Private Residence is reflected in every guestroom. complimentary bottled water, bathrobes, internet access – wireless (complimentary), hair dryer, television LCD/plasma screen are just some of the facilities at your disposal. Besides, the hotel's host of recreational offerings ensures you have plenty to do during your stay. Discover an engaging blend of professional service and a wide array of features at An Lam Saigon River Private Residence.

What's nearby? / Directions



Directions
Tan Son Nhat INT’ Airport - Resort :
From Tan Son Nhat Airport, An Lam Saigon River can be reached by getting straight on Hong Ha Street then turning right to Nguyen Thai Son Street. On the way, you will pass Pham Ngu Lao Street and reach the big roundabout with 5 crossroads (called Nga 5 Chuong Cho). Here, you turn to Nguyen Oanh Street and get straight that way until you reach another roundabout called Nga Tu Ga. Then, you turn right to Highway 1A and get straight to pass by Binh Phuoc Overpass. Then you turn left to Highway 13, get straight you will pass by Hanh Phuc Hospital (on the left) and heading straight to Phuong Nam resort (on the right). From here, you will find the sign of An Lam Saigon River on the left. Please turn to the alley and you are welcome to An Lam Saigon River.

District 1 – Resort :
From District 1, An Lam Saigon River can be reached by getting straight on Nguyen Thi Minh Khai Street and then Xo Viet Nghe Tinh Street. On that way, you will pass by Thi Nghe Bridge, the big roundabout called Hang Xanh, Ben Xe Mien Dong (Eastern Bus Station) and Binh Trieu Bridge. Here, you’re on Highway 13. Getting straight on this highway, you will pass by Hanh Phuc Hospital (on the left). From here, you will find the sign of An Lam Saigon River on the left. Please turn to the alley and you are welcome to An Lam Saigon River.

How to get there?
An Lam Saigon River can be headed by Boat or Car.
By Car: From Airport or Center
By Boat: From Bach Dang Pier
